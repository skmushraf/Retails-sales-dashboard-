📊 **Sales Performance Tracker | Power BI Dashboard**
 📌 Overview
The Sales Performance Tracker is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed using e-commerce sales data to monitor business performance across products, customer segments, regions, and time periods. It provides a comprehensive view of revenue trends, profitability, and top-performing products, enabling data-driven decision-making and business analysis.

## 📈 Dashboard Insights
This dashboard provides insights into:
Overall Business Performance
Total Sales: 14.92M
Total Profit: 1.52M

Sales Distribution by Product Category
Technology contributes the highest share of sales (40.12%), followed by Furniture (34.72%) and Office Supplies (25.16%).

1.Profit by Customer Segment

  * Corporate customers generate the highest profit.
  * Consumer and Small Business segments show comparable profitability, while Home Office contributes slightly less.

2.Monthly Sales Trends

  * Sales fluctuate throughout the year, with peaks observed during October and November.
  * September records the lowest monthly sales, indicating seasonal variations.

3.Regional Performance

  * The West region leads in sales with **3.6M (24.1%)**, followed by Ontario and Prairie.
  * Yukon contributes the smallest share among the regions.

4.Top Performing Products

  * The dashboard highlights the top-selling products, with the leading product generating 276K in sales.

🎯 Dashboard Objectives

* Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing product categories and products.
* Analyze profitability across customer segments.
* Track monthly sales trends and seasonality.
* Compare sales performance across regions.
* Provide actionable insights for business growth and strategic planning.
